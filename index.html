Ecosystem

One brand. Three project pillars.

LifeVerse RP, LifeCoin, and Lifetime each serve a distinct role while remaining connected through one ecosystem vision.

LifeVerse RP

LifeVerse RP is a next-generation role-playing game built around immersive multiplayer systems, player progression, property and business ownership, and a dual-currency economy.

  • Persistent role-playing world
  • Bulk + LifeCoin economic model
  • Blockchain-linked value layer
LifeVerse RP Discord

LifeCoin (LFC)

LifeCoin is an independent Proof-of-Work blockchain built for fast, low-cost transactions, with 100% mining distribution, no premine, and no ICO.

  • Scrypt proof-of-work
  • 84,000,000 total supply
  • Independent blockchain architecture
LifeCoin Discord

Lifetime

Lifetime is the ecosystem’s clothing and lifestyle brand, positioned at the intersection of digital identity, premium fashion, and physical brand expression.

  • Fashion and lifestyle direction
  • LifeCoin payment utility
  • Ecosystem-linked brand identity
