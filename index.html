Ecosystem

One brand. Three core projects.

The LifeVerse ecosystem is built around a gaming project, a blockchain project, and a lifestyle brand.

LifeVerse RP

LifeVerse RP is a next-generation role-playing game designed around immersive multiplayer systems, player progression, property and business ownership, and a dual-currency model.

  • Role-playing gameplay with persistent world systems
  • Bulk + LifeCoin dual-currency structure
  • Blockchain-linked value layer
LifeVerse RP Discord

LifeCoin (LFC)

LifeCoin is an independent Proof-of-Work blockchain built for fast, low-cost transactions with a fair-launch model and 100% mining distribution.

  • Scrypt proof-of-work
  • 84,000,000 total supply
  • No premine and no ICO
LifeCoin Discord

Lifetime

Lifetime is the ecosystem’s clothing and lifestyle brand, created to connect digital identity with real-world fashion, accessories, and brand expression.

  • Fashion and lifestyle direction
  • LifeCoin payment utility
  • Ecosystem-linked brand identity
