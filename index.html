Blockchain project

LifeCoin (LFC)

An independent decentralized Proof-of-Work blockchain designed for fair mining distribution, fast blocks, and practical ecosystem utility.

Technical profile

  • Base code: Litecoin Core
  • Consensus: Scrypt Proof-of-Work
  • Block time: 60 seconds
  • Total supply: 84,000,000 LFC
  • Distribution: 100% mining
  • No premine, no ICO, no founder allocation

Why it matters

  • Fair access for miners
  • Fast confirmations for daily use
  • Designed to integrate with LifeVerse RP and Lifetime
  • Built for accessibility and real-world utility

About the company

LifeVerse Technologies Ltd

A digital innovation company founded by Fortune Godson and based in Anambra State, Nigeria. The company focuses on building a unified ecosystem that combines gaming, cryptocurrency, and lifestyle branding.

Mission

To create a digital ecosystem where users can play, earn, and live through integrated gaming, finance, and lifestyle platforms, bridging virtual experiences and real-world value.

Vision

To become a global leader in metaverse gaming, decentralized finance, and digital lifestyle branding where digital and physical realities blend to create new opportunities.
